What we do

Our Products Are Farm-Fresh and Pure

At GreenWay Apiaries, we offer a variety of honey and bee-related products that are harvested directly from our own hives, ensuring quality and purity in everything we produce.

Spring Honey

Our light and delicate spring honey is harvested in July and is perfect for a mild, floral flavor.

Autumn Honey

This darker, more robust honey is harvested in late summer or early autumn, ideal for those who love a stronger honey flavor.

100% Beeswax Candles

Our pure beeswax candles are made from our hives, offering a natural and long-lasting burn.

Propolis Tincture

Our propolis tincture is made from the resinous material collected by bees, known for its potential health benefits.

Hand and Skin Cream

Our all-natural hand and skin cream is made from beeswax and other nourishing ingredients to keep your skin soft and hydrated.

Comb Honey (Coming 2025)

Our comb honey, launching in 2025, is harvested directly from the hive and provides an entirely raw, natural honey experience.

OUR PRICING

Fair & Transparent Pricing

At GreenWay Apiaries, we offer competitive prices that are often lower than those of honey packers because we produce all our goods directly from our own beehives. This helps reduce our bottom line while ensuring you receive high-quality, farm-fresh products. Our pricing reflects the love and care that goes into every jar of honey, beeswax candle, and more.

HAVE A QUESTION?

Get answers to FAQs about our products

What makes GreenWay Apiaries' honey different from others?

We produce all of our honey and bee products directly from our own beehives, ensuring we know exactly what goes into each product. Unlike many competitors who source materials externally, we control the entire process, from hive to jar.

Where can I buy your products?

Our products are available at a handful of retail locations in Northeast Pennsylvania, at farmers markets, and local events. We are also adding online sales soon, so you can order directly from our website and have it delivered to your doorstep.

What is propolis tincture, and how is it used?

Propolis is a resinous material collected by bees from trees and plants. Itâ€™s used in the hive for its antimicrobial properties. Our propolis tincture can be used for its potential health benefits, including supporting the immune system.

Are your bees local to Pennsylvania?

Yes, we breed Pennsylvania local stock of bees that are known for their gentle nature and resistance to varroa mites. These bees are uniquely suited to thrive in our local environment and help maintain healthy colonies.

Do You Use Any Additives in Your Honey?

No, all of our honey is 100% natural and raw, with no additives or preservatives. We believe in providing pure, farm-fresh honey straight from the hive to your table.

About GreenWay Apiaries

Dedicated to Sustainable Beekeeping & Pure Honey Products

Founded in 2012, GreenWay Apiaries has been at the forefront of sustainable beekeeping practices in Eastern and Northern Pennsylvania. We strive to provide farm-to-table honey and bee products, all while focusing on breeding local bee stock that can combat the varroa mite problem through genetic resistance.

100% Natural Honey Products

Our honey, beeswax candles, propolis tincture, and skin creams are made from the raw materials we produce ourselves, ensuring complete quality control from start to finish.

Years of Beekeeping Expertise

Owner Devon Paderewski has over a decade of experience, working with both commercial beekeeping operations and now focusing on local beekeeping and research in Pennsylvania. His work has been recognized by PennState and Purdue University for its innovation in bee genetics and sustainability.
